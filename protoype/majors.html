Pick your Major








General information will include a discussion board for students to talk about their classes and the major as a whole.
Pinned and upvoted notes can be left to inform all students in the major about upcoming events, internships, professor feedback.
Individual classes can contain information about pre-requisite courses, what technical knowledge you should have before taking the course,
what professors have taught in the past and teach currently. A feature we are interested in is collecting the RateMyProfessors ratings
on a class by class basis, rather than for professors in total.
Individual classes can also contain information for textbooks, required tools for the course, and student enjoyment of the class,
maybe akin to how students leave professor feedback at the end of each term.
